Workers Compensation Payroll Update Form

Please use this form to provide your organization’s estimated annual payroll for each group of employees based on the type of work they do. In Workers Compensation, these job types are called class codes. Putting payroll under the correct job type helps make sure your premium is based on the right kind of work your employees perform and helps avoid being overcharged during the policy term or at audit. You may refer to your Workers Compensation Renewal Summary for guidance on class codes and estimated payroll. If you are not sure which class code applies, select “Not sure” and provide a brief description of the job duties so our team can assist.
